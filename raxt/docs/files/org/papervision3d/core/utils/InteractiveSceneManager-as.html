virtualMouse

public var virtualMouse: VirtualMouse

VirtualMouse is used with faceLevelMode of ISM or DO3D’s.  Its a virtual mouse that causes the objects in your materials movieclip containers to fire off their mouse events such as click, over, out, release, press etc </p> <p> Using these events requires you only to do what you normally do - establish listeners with your objects like you normally would, and you’ll receive them!
